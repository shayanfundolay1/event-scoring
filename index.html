About Us

Turn to Event Scoring System, if you are looking for an easy way to track and display scores for racing events. We offer user-friendly software that you can utilize when posting scores for your events. Using our scoring system allows the spectators of your event to view race results as well as lap times with just one click on your website or viewing on their phone.
Our business was created out of the desire to find a better way to score race events, After racing numerous AMA District 11 events , National Enduro’ s and finishing the 1994 ISDE with a bronze medal on a club team, Event Scoring System was started to make scoring races easier. Event scoring System has scored numerous ISDE qualifiers in the early years and found our niche scoring Hare Scramble and Cross country type events. ESS ( Event Scoring System) who provides our own scoring solutions is also a partner with Sport-tag Racing transponders, for Scoring applications that require an Active transponder for faster type races such as Desert Truck races
When you use our portable scoring system, you will also have access to technical support. You are most welcome to contact us anytime if you are experiencing troubles using the software. A proud member of American Motorcyclist Association™ (AMA) and The Alternative Board™ (TAB), we stop at nothing to provide you with first-rate products and services.
